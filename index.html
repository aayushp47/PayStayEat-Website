About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2024, our PG and Mess service has grown from a modest beginning into a premier facility in the heart of the city. We pride ourselves on providing comfortable living and gourmet dining experiences tailored to students and working professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a home away from home where residents can live, study, and relax in comfort. We aim to provide an environment that supports our residents' educational journeys and professional growth with state-of-the-art amenities and a supportive community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the types of rooms available and their prices?

We offer Single, Double, and Triple rooms ranging from Rs.3000,4000,4500 respectively per month, inclusive of all amenities.

What are the mess timings and are there any meal options?

Our mess is open from 7 AM to 9 PM. We offer vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian meal options, with meals served three times a day.

How can I book a room and what is the payment policy?

You can book a room through our website or by contacting us directly. We require a one-month deposit and the first month’s rent upfront.

Do you have policies for late payments or cancellations?

Yes, late payments may incur a fee, and cancellations must be made 30 days in advance for a full deposit refund.

What amenities are included with the accommodations?

All rooms come with Wi-Fi, laundry services, room cleaning, and access to the mess hall.

Is there security on the premises?

Yes, we ensure full-time security with surveillance cameras and security personnel on duty 24/7.
